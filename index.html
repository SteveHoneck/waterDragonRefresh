Welcome!

This site was created after I learned the basics of Bootstrap v4.6, Scss, and some very basic jQuery.

The goal of this site was to re-imagine the layout of Tricia's Water Dragon Care Page (a website I referred to often in the late 90s), while practicing the use of BS, jQ, and Scss.

All section headers are from Tricia's Water Dragon Care Page. The text content associated with the headers is equal in length to that of the original website. Lorem ipsum was used for the text content due to copyrighting of the original website. Doing this allowed me to experiment with the ways the large amount of text content could be displayed using Bootstrap.

The entire original website was not mapped over. I only added the majority of what I found useful back when I used the original website. There is still plenty that I want to change around, but this is the starting point.

Thanks for looking, and feedback is always welcome!
